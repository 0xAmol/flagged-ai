When nothing is found, we say exactly that

A clean scan means we found no artifacts. It is not a certificate of authenticity, and Artifake will never give one. Good AI content can pass a scan, which is why every check also consults the community record: content flagged and confirmed by crowd votes that no single scan could settle.
